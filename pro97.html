iPad Pro 9.7 GSM to WiFi converter

On the Model A1674 delete resistor #1 and #3. Optional: palce 1 resistor or make jumper on place #2. iPad Pro 9.7 4G will enter DFU mode. Connect the iPad Pro 9.7 4G to your PC or Mac. After that you need to restore the iPad Pro 9.7 4G through iTunes with firmware of Wi-Fi model and activate using the official method. Turn off the automatic firmware update: Settings \ iTunes Store, App Store \ Automatic download \ Updates.
