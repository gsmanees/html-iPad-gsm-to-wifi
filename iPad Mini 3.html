iPad Mini 3 GSM to WiFi converter

On the iPad mini 3 4G Model A1600 Remove Resistor #4. Make a jumper on position #2. Or instead of a jumper on position #2 you can solder in resistor #4 on position #2. iPad mini 3 4G will enter DFU mode. Connect the iPad mini 3 4G to your PC or Mac. After that you need to restore the iPad mini 3 4G through iTunes with firmware of Wi-Fi model and activate using the official method. Turn off the automatic firmware update: Settings \ iTunes Store, App Store \ Automatic download \ Updates.
