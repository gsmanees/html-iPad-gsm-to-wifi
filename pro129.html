iPad Pro 12.9 GSM to WiFi converter

On the Model A1652 delete 1 resistor. iPad Pro 12.9 4G will enter DFU mode. Connect the iPad Pro 12.9 4G to your PC or Mac. After that you need to restore the iPad Pro 12.9 4G through iTunes with firmware of Wi-Fi model and activate using the official method. Turn off the automatic firmware update: Settings \ iTunes Store, App Store \ Automatic download \ Updates.
