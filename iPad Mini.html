iPad Mini GSM to WiFi converter

A1454 remove the resistor R1204 and make jumper on place of R1205. A1455 (MM) remove resistors R1204, R1205 and make jumper on place of R1205. iPad will enter DFU mode. Connect the tablet to your PC or Mac. After that you need to restore the tablet through iTunes with firmware of Wi-Fi model and activate using the official method.
