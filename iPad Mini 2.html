iPad Mini 2 GSM to WiFi converter

A1490 Remove resistors #3 and #4. Make a jumper on position #2. Or instead of a jumper on position #2 you can solder in resistor #3 on position #2. Tablet will enter DFU mode. Connect the tablet to your PC or Mac After that you need to restore the tablet through iTunes with firmware of Wi-Fi model and activate using the official method Turn off the automatic firmware update: Settings \ iTunes Store, App Store \ Automatic download \ Updates.
