iPad Mini 4 GSM to WiFi converter

On the Model A1550 delete 1 resistor. iPad mini 3 4G will enter DFU mode. Connect the iPad mini 4 4G to your PC or Mac. After that you need to restore the iPad mini 3 4G through iTunes with firmware of Wi-Fi model and activate using the official method. Turn off the automatic firmware update: Settings \ iTunes Store, App Store \ Automatic download \ Updates.
